Delete Your Account

App: Deeper • Developer: ICARI Institute • Updated: 14 Aug 2025

How to Request Deletion

To delete your account and associated data, please email us from the same email address used in the app:

Email: grant.fullston@gmail.com

Subject line: Account Deletion Request – Deeper

In the email, include: your full name (optional) and a short confirmation that you want your Deeper account deleted.

We will confirm receipt and complete deletion within 7 business days.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your Deeper account profile and identifiers stored in our database
  • Your app activity/content associated with your account (where applicable)
  • OAuth tokens/links between your Google account and Deeper

What May Be Retained (and Why)

Some data may be retained for legal, security, or audit purposes, including:

  • Minimal transactional/system logs required to comply with law
  • Billing records (if any) for accounting and tax compliance

Retention period: up to 7 years where legally required (e.g., accounting records). Retained data is not used to operate your account.

If You Signed In with Google

Deeper uses Google OAuth for sign-in. Deleting your Deeper account will also remove the association between your Google account and Deeper within our systems. You may additionally review connected apps in your Google Account: myaccount.google.com/permissions.

Questions

If you have any questions before requesting deletion, email grant.fullston@gmail.com.
